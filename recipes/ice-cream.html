Ice Cream

Description

Cool, creamy, and made just for the best pups!

Ingrediants

  • 4 Bananas
  • Half a cup of Greek or plain yogurt
  • Half a cup of all natural peanut butter

Steps

  1. Peel and slice bananas
  2. Transfer all ingrediants to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy
  3. Pour mixture into airtight container and freeze for 4 to 6 hours.
