Mash Sweet Potatos



Description

This deliciouses recipe of plain but simple mash sweet potatos will make any dogo go crazy. The dish is only for the best. It is a dish of mighty warriors.


Ingrediants

    1 Sweet potato

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the sweet potato
  2. Dice it into cubes
  3. Boil it in 1 liter of water
  4. Check the potato for softness after 20 minutes (stab a diced potato with a fork)
